Frequently Asked Questions
What is Klassy?
Klassy is a web and mobile portal designed to futfill all your local lifestyle needs. You can read more about it on our About Us page.
Where do you get your information from?
We have a business development team that is always on the go gathering information and fostering new partnerships with local businesses. Once the business is listed on our platform, they are the sole managers of their profile on Klassy.
Are all businesses around me listed?
If the business is in the Lifestyle sector, it is our priority to get them onboard. As a new startup, we may not have all the businesses in your locality listed. In that case, you can request a listing by dropping a mail at contact@klassy.in
Why do you require login from Facebook?
It removes the hassle for you to create another account and remember your login details. Plus, using Facebook we get your email and name without having to ask you for it. We never post on your behalf without asking you.
Are all product listings up to date?
Business owners are in complete control of their online profile. They decide which products to list and which to not. We constantly stay in touch with our partners to make sure their details are up to date. In case you wish to verify, you can use the 'message' feature to message a business owner about the same. If you face any issues with a particular store or business, you can contact us at contact@klassy.in
I claimed a coupon on Klassy, until when is it valid?
Some coupons have an end date specified on them. If the coupon does not have an end date specified it is a perrenial coupon that would work all year round.
How do I redeem a coupon claimed on Klassy?
Coupon redemptions can only be done through the Klassy mobile app. When making an in-store purchase, show the business owner your coupon to redeem it.
How can I purchase a product through Klassy?
Currently we do not support online payments but we will soon - both pickup and delivery. At the moment, you can contact a business owner and ask to reserve a product, and go pick it up later. Don't forget to redeem any coupons which you might have gotten through the app!
How can I request to list my business?
You can submit your details here and someone from our team will get back to you for further communication.
